Welcome!

Thank you for stopping by. It is my hope that in the next little while you will be curious, and hopefully even inspired by some new thought, personal goals, and the underlying pursuits we all share that go beyond simply displaying information. My goal is to really understand a community of people. I wish to understand their personality, their values, their emotions, and goals. It’s important for me to understand what those traits mean when a person promotes a message from themselves to the public. I believe that great design happens when the design communicates the traits of the promoters, the designers, and the traits of the public viewing it. When the larger population agrees, respects, and engages in the positive values promoted through a message, the designer's goal is complete. The role I pursue in the whole design process is to communicate the concepts and takeaways one person wants to share with the other. Welcome to my Graphic Design portfolio.

About

I’m a young adult at the age of 23 named Sebastian. I am someone who is constantly growing and expanding my knowledge base and am pursuing to take on new endeavours. I am a recent graduate from the Graphic Design program at Algonquin College and during my time there and beyond I put care and attention into the projects I work on. I am at the stage where I am trying different possibilities that are out there and am figuring out what excites me the most. Simple illustration, motion graphics, and interactive UI have really grabbed me and now I am devoting more of my attention into working with motion graphics. I really enjoy the type of work that is out there and that is available for Graphic Designers to work on.
